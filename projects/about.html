About the Artist

Adam “Sussy” Barnes is an indie artist/DJ based in Charlotte, NC. His sound blends downtempo textures with high-energy club cuts. He’s performed at college venues and late-night events across the region.

Influences

  • Kaytranada • Four Tet • Jamie xx
  • UK Garage, House, Lo-Fi Hip-Hop
  • ’90s R&B samples and modular synths

Highlights

  • Opened for local headliners at The Underground (2025)
  • Self-released EP “Night Drive” (2024)
  • Campus radio guest mixes
