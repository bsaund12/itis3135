Bashful Shark — About

Why “Bashful”? Not everyone wants the loud, high-pressure surf shop vibe. Bashful Shark is for first-timers and ocean lovers who want to learn at their own pace, grab simple gear, and catch a sunset session without the fuss.

What we do: We keep a tiny, easy-to-choose inventory so decisions feel simple. Our four staples are Mini-Boards & Foamies, Shark Tooth Necklaces, Shark Bite Wax, and Wave Starter Lessons. That’s it — small shop, big smiles.

Our vibe

Think golden hour on the beach — warm, relaxed, and welcoming. We keep things small on purpose so you can get to the water faster, whether you’re renting a foamie or grabbing a necklace as a keepsake.
